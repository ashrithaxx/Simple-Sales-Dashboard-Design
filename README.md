# Simple-Sales-Dashboard-Design
## Task 8  

## Dataset  
- Superstore Sales Dataset  

## Tools Used  
- Power BI  

## Visualizations Created  
- Sales Over Time (Line Chart)  
- Sales by Region (Bar Chart)  
- Sales by Category (Donut Chart)  
- Region Slicer (Interactive Filter)    

## Insights  
- West region generated the highest overall sales, followed closely by East.  
- Sales show a clear upward trend over the years, with peak performance observed in 2017.  
- Technology is the leading category, contributing the largest share of total sales (~36%).  
- South region recorded the lowest sales, indicating potential growth opportunities.  
- There is a noticeable performance gap between top-performing and lower-performing regions.  

## Outcome  
Built an interactive and visually clean sales dashboard to analyze performance by time, region, and category.  
Gained understanding of data visualization, dashboard layout design, and business insight extraction using Power BI.
